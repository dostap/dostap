About Me

An aspiring software developer who dreams in code and all the genius software applications I will build.

I am currently finishing a college certificate program in software development, as I have deemed my Bachelor degree in engineering insufficient in my aspirations to conquer at least a small part of the world.

Why blog? Has it not gone out of vogue?

I don't think it has, I love this medium of self-expression. I might prefer podcasts (there is an odd feeling of kinship that you start to develop to strangers if you listen to them tell you enough stories...)

The reason for this blog, at this point in my life, is to follow my career change journey into the world of programming. Explaining the new concepts I learn would be a great way to cement my new knowledge, ensure my full comprehension of it, and, hopefully, to help some other soul along their learning journey.

Blogging has many other benefits, and some of the most compelling ones, I have found here, at Becoming Minimalist. Check it out if curious.
